## CRITICAL Iran missile report sends crypto markets sharply lower
Iran's Fars news agency claimed two missiles hit a U.S. warship. Oil spiked 5%. Bitcoin dropped $1,500 — from $80,594 to $79,000. Then the U.S. denied it. Everything bounced back.

This is the fifth geopolitical flash-crash in 2026. The pattern: unconfirmed report → instant dump → denial → recovery. Recovery speed is improving each time, which means liquidity is deepening.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/04/iran-missile-report-sends-bitcoin-back-to-usd79-000-with-eth-sol-doge-sharply-lower)

## IMPORTANT Lawyer seeks to seize 30,765 ETH from Arbitrum DAO
A lawyer appeared on Arbitrum DAO forums to seize 30,765 ETH (frozen after last month's rsETH exploit) for victims of North Korean terrorist acts. The legal hook: Lazarus Group was allegedly behind the exploit, and a NY restraining notice could block Arbitrum from releasing the funds.

Uncharted territory. DAOs have no legal personality, no jurisdiction, no CEO to serve. If a court enforces this, every creditor and government agency gets a playbook for going after DAO treasuries.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/04/laywer-pops-up-on-arbitrum-dao-forums-seeking-funds-for-victims-of-decades-old-north-korean-terrorist-acts)

## POSITIVE Bitcoin ETF inflows recovery shows institutional demand remains strong
ETF inflows are picking back up after a decline. Not yet at Q1 peaks, but the trend is encouraging. Institutional money didn't leave — it was waiting for a better entry. With BTC back above $78K, expect flows to accelerate.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/04/the-bitcoin-etf-recovery-in-flows-is-real-it-is-just-not-complete-yet)

## IMPORTANT Coinbase boosts Solana trading with DFlow integration
Coinbase added DFlow as its primary Solana router — eight times fewer failed trades. Solana's uptime has improved, but reliability on exchanges has been the real bottleneck. This fixes the user experience problem that kept institutional volume on Ethereum.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/05/04/coinbase-boosts-solana-trading-with-dflow-integration)

## POSITIVE Veteran trader sees bitcoin hitting $250,000 by 2029
Peter Brandt called for $250K BTC by 2029 — but only after a bottoming process that could stretch to September 2026. Long-term bull, short-term patience. The prediction adds weight to the "lower for longer before higher" camp.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/04/veteran-trader-peter-brandt-sees-bitcoin-hitting-usd250-000-but-only-after-a-bottom-later-this-year)
