## 1. CLARITY Act Stablecoin Yields
Finalized rules allow "bona fide" stablecoin transactions while blocking bank-like yield products. Senate cleared the compromise, removing a significant legislative hurdle for crypto market structure.

April saw record $2.44B net inflows into spot Bitcoin ETFs. AIMCo, the Canadian pension giant, re-entered via MicroStrategy and is sitting on $69M in unrealized gains. Bitcoin price holding above $78K.
