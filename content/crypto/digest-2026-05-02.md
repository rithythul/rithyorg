## 1. CLARITY Act Stablecoin Yields
Finalized rules allow "bona fide" stablecoin transactions but block bank-like yield products. Senate cleared the compromise — biggest legislative hurdle for crypto market structure is now behind us.

## 2. Institutional Bitcoin Flows
April net inflows into spot BTC ETFs hit $2.44B — a record. AIMCo, Canada's largest pension fund, re-entered via Strategy and is up $69M. Bitcoin holding above $78K.

## 3. Ethereum Foundation Sales
Another 10,000 ETH sold to BitMine (~$23M), totaling ~$47M in liquidations this year. The pace is consistent — roughly $15M/month in OTC deals.

## 4. Tether Financials
