## 🔴 Brazil Bans Crypto for Cross-Border Payments
Brazil's central bank banned stablecoins and crypto from cross-border payment settlement. The ban targets fintechs and payment firms specifically — individual investors can still buy and hold. This is a direct shot at the crypto remittance corridor in LatAm's largest economy. Other emerging market central banks will be watching this closely.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/05/02/brazil-s-central-bank-bans-stablecoin-and-crypto-settlement-in-cross-border-payments)

## 🔴 $292M DeFi Hack — Biggest of 2026
A multi-chain exploit drained $292M from a lending protocol, making it the largest crypto hack this year. Auditors missed the flash loan attack vector. The hack is forcing a broader conversation about DeFi security as Wall Street continues moving onchain. Industry insiders say risk models need fundamental rethinking.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/05/02/the-usd292m-crypto-hack-exposed-defi-s-weak-spots-here-s-what-must-change-insiders-say)

## 🟡 CLARITY Act Clears Senate Yield Hurdle
The crypto industry backed a yield compromise in the CLARITY Act, pushing Senate Banking for markup. The agreement requires firms to restructure reward programs from "buy and hold" to "buy and use" models. Crypto Council for Innovation raised concerns over the broad prohibition scope. Bitcoin climbed back above $78K on the news alongside a new S&P 500 record.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/05/02/crypto-industry-backs-clarity-act-yield-compromise-pushes-senate-banking-for-markup)

## 🟡 Bitcoin eCash Fork Draws Developer Warnings
Paul Sztorc's eCash fork proposal for Bitcoin is facing pushback from developers who warn about user risk, uneven distribution, and philosophical tension. The airdrop-based approach is being called "hazardous" by industry figures. Debate highlights the ongoing tension between Bitcoin innovation and network conservatism.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/05/02/bitcoin-s-hazardous-airdrop-why-developers-are-warning-against-paul-sztorc-s-ecash-fork)

## 🟢 Prediction Markets Hit $240B Industry
Prediction markets are shedding their "casino" reputation and becoming mainstream news-tracking tools. A Bitget and Polymarket report shows the space has grown to $240B driven by retail users trading on crypto, politics, and more. The shift mirrors how traditional betting markets became legitimate financial instruments.

Source: [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/05/02/prediction-markets-are-ditching-the-casino-label-to-become-a-regular-part-of-how-people-track-the-news)
